## 05.01.07 – Threat Level / Numeric Risk Score

### 1️⃣ Threat Level Assessment

| Criteria                           | Observation                                                                 | Threat Level |
| --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- | ------------ |
| Attack Type                        | RDP Brute Force                                                              | High         |
| Attacker Origin                     | External IP 192.168.1.106                                                   | Medium       |
| Targeted Accounts                   | `testuser`, SYSTEM, DWM-2                                                   | High         |
| Logon Attempts                      | Multiple failed logins followed by SYSTEM/DWM logins                         | High         |
| Post-Login Activity                 | Script execution (splunk-powershell.exe) and credential access (lsass.exe)  | High         |

> **Insight:** The combination of brute force attempts, post-login credential access, and session manipulation justifies a **High threat level**.
