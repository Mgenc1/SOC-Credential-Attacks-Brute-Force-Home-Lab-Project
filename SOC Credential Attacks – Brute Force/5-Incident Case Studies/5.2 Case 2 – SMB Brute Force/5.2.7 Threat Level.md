### Risk Score

| Metric | Value |
|------|------|
| Threat Level | High |
| Risk Score | 8 / 10 |
| Attack Type | SMB Brute Force |
| MITRE ATT&CK Technique | T1110 – Brute Force |

### Justification

The threat level is considered **High** due to the following factors:

- Multiple failed login attempts from a single source host  
- Successful authentication following repeated failures  
- Direct targeting of a remote authentication service (SMB)  
- Confirmation of network activity related to the attack through Sysmon logs  

Without proper mitigation, this type of activity could enable attackers to gain unauthorized access and potentially move laterally within the network.
