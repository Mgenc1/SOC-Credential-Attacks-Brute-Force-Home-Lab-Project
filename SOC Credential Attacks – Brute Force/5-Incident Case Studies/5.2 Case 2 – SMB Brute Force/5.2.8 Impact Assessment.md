### Potential Security Impact

| Impact Category | Assessment | Description |
|-----------------|------------|-------------|
| Unauthorized Access | High | Successful authentication (Event ID 4624) indicates that the attacker obtained valid credentials and accessed the system. |
| Credential Exposure | Medium | Access through SMB may allow attackers to interact with authentication mechanisms and potentially harvest credentials. |
| Lateral Movement | High | Compromised credentials can be reused to access additional systems within the internal network. |
| Data Exposure | Medium | Access to SMB shares could allow attackers to view or copy sensitive files stored on the target system. |
| System Integrity | Medium | Attackers with authenticated access may execute commands or modify system resources. |

### Impact Summary

The successful authentication observed during the attack indicates that the attacker was able to gain access to the target system.  
This type of access can enable further malicious activities such as lateral movement, credential harvesting, or unauthorized access to internal resources.

If left unmitigated, the compromise of one account through SMB authentication could potentially lead to broader network compromise.
