## 1. Failed Login Attempts

**Goal:** Identify brute-force attempts.

```spl
index=main sourcetype=WinEventLog:Security EventCode=4625
| stats count by Account_Name, Source_Network_Address
| sort -count
```

**Explanation:**

* `EventCode=4625` → Failed login
* Aggregates **user accounts** and **source IPs**
* Sorting by count highlights **top attacking IPs** or most targeted users

---

## 2. Successful Logins

**Goal:** Identify which logins succeeded after failures (potential account compromise)

```spl
index=main sourcetype=WinEventLog:Security EventCode=4624
| stats count by Account_Name, Source_Network_Address
| sort -count
```

**Explanation:**

* `EventCode=4624` → Successful login
* Correlate with failed attempts to detect **brute-force success**
* Useful for **timeline analysis** and cross-case correlation

---

## 3. Post-login Process Activity

**Goal:** Detect suspicious processes executed after successful logins

```spl
index=sysmon sourcetype=WinEventLog:Microsoft-Windows-Sysmon/Operational EventCode=1
| stats count by Image, User, ProcessId
| sort -count
```

**Explanation:**

* `EventCode=1` → Process creation events
* Shows which processes were executed **after successful authentication**
* Helps detect **post-compromise activity** (e.g., credential dumps, malicious scripts)

---

## 4. Targeted Users & IOC Extraction

**Goal:** Extract IOCs (IPs, users, processes) for dashboards and MITRE mapping

```spl
index=main OR index=sysmon
| eval Attacking_IP=coalesce(Source_Network_Address, SourceIp)
| eval Target_User=Account_Name
| stats values(Attacking_IP) as Attacking_IP, values(Target_User) as Target_User, values(Image) as Process
| table Attacking_IP, Target_User, Process
```

**Explanation:**

* Combines **Security logs** (failed/successful logins) and **Sysmon logs** (post-login activity)
* Produces a **table of indicators** for IOC tracking and Splunk dashboards
